Etch-a-Sketch

Here you can draw what you want.
Enjoy!

  • Each "pixel" color will be random.
  • The button "reset-grid" will make the grid white, wiping every magnificent previous drawing of yours.
  • The button 'Set # of "pixels"' allows you to set the amount of "pixels" constituting the side of the grid (which is, believe it or not, a square).
  • It is advised to not set # beyond 100 (since even 100 might take some time to render [10 seconds on my computer]).
  • If you feel like a true artist being able to draw something ""realistic"" and not have the excuse "that's because it's a low resolution", set # to 100, wait for rendering (takes around 10 seconds for me) and enjoy your "ultra-hd 4kk res"!
